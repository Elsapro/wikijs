# The 5 Stages of Grief & Loss
-  The 5 stages of grief and loss are: 
		1. Denial and isolation
		2. Anger
		3. Bargaining
		4. Depression
		5. Acceptance 
		People who are grieving do not necessarily go through the stages in the same order or experience all of them.
